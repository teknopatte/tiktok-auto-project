Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

1. Overview

TikTok Auto Project is a private local software tool used to manage video preparation, publishing, and analytics workflows. This policy explains what information may be processed by the tool.

2. Information Processed

The tool may process video metadata, local file paths, publishing status, analytics data such as views or likes, and account authorization tokens granted by the user through official platform authorization flows.

3. How Information Is Used

Information is used to prepare videos, publish authorized content, track publishing status, retrieve analytics, and improve local workflow decisions such as selecting background videos.

4. Storage

Data is stored locally by the project owner unless the owner chooses to connect external services. API credentials and tokens should be stored securely and must not be committed to public repositories.

5. Sharing

The tool does not sell personal data. Information may be transmitted to TikTok or other connected services only as needed to perform authorized API actions requested by the user.

6. User Control

Users can revoke TikTok access from their TikTok account settings or delete local configuration and state files from the project directory.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the project owner at contact@aemour.com.
